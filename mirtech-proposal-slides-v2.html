What we see

Your workflow has outgrown the tools running it.

Context collapse
Every tool switch means re-explaining from scratch. Claude, Gemini, GPT — each conversation starts cold. The knowledge your team built disappears between steps.
Non-linear work, linear tools
One topic branches into 5 angles × 5 audiences × 5 formats. No tool holds that tree. You're managing the structure in your head and across tabs.
Human review without a home
Every stage needs a judgment call before moving forward. But the tools don't support that rhythm. Review happens around the workflow, not inside it.
Copy-paste as infrastructure
The handoff between steps is manual. That's not a process gap — it's a ceiling. It limits how fast your team can scale and how much you can delegate.
We've seen this pattern enough to have named it. illumi is built specifically for branching, context-heavy, human-reviewed content workflows. We're confident we can fix this.

Phase 2 · Post deep dive · LOI on agreed scope

Scope agreed.
We formalise it and build.

What triggers the LOI
The deep dive is done. Scope is defined. Both sides agree on what is being built and what success looks like. Then we sign.
What the LOI covers
Pilot success criteria, build scope, and Mirtech's commitment to the agreed scope for the pilot period.
Pilot build begins
Mirtech tests first against criteria set in Phase 1. You define what success looks like — we build to meet it.
What it is not
Not a binding purchase order. Intent, not obligation. If the pilot does not hit criteria, we renegotiate together — not unilaterally.
✦
The sequence: deep dive first → scope agreed → LOI signed → build begins. No LOI before both sides know what they are committing to.
